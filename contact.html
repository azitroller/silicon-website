FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with us.

How quickly can you respond to a service request?

For existing customers with support contracts, we typically respond to service requests within 2-4 hours during business hours, with emergency support available 24/7 for critical systems.

Do you offer installation services for purchased products?

Yes, we provide professional installation and setup services for all hardware and software products we sell, ensuring they're properly integrated into your existing infrastructure.

Do you offer warranty and post-sales support?

Absolutely. All our products come with manufacturer warranties, and we offer various support packages ranging from basic email support to comprehensive managed services with on-site technical assistance.

Can you provide customized solutions for specific industries?

Yes, we specialize in tailoring technology solutions for specific industry requirements, particularly in banking, telecommunications, healthcare, and education sectors. Our consultants work closely with you to understand your unique needs.
