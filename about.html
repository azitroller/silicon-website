About Silicon

Our story, mission, and the team powering innovation.

Our Story

Silicon Computer and Communications PLC has been at the forefront of technology innovation since our founding in 1995.

Starting as a small hardware provider, we've grown into a full-service technology solutions company serving clients across multiple industries and geographies.

Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions with exceptional service, building long-term relationships with our clients and partners.

Our Mission

To empower businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and competitive advantage.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner for technology solutions, recognized for our expertise, reliability, and customer-centric approach.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

Key moments that have shaped our growth and success over the years.

1995: Foundation

Silicon Computer and Communications was founded with a focus on providing hardware solutions to local businesses.

2001: Network Services Launch

Expanded service offerings to include networking solutions, becoming one of the first companies to offer enterprise-grade networks locally.

2005: Corporate Expansion

Incorporated as Silicon Computer and Communications PLC and expanded operations to serve clients nationwide.

2010: Banking Solutions

Developed specialized banking hardware and software solutions, becoming the preferred provider for financial institutions.

2015: Cloud Services

Launched comprehensive cloud services offerings, helping clients migrate to and manage cloud infrastructure.

2020: Digital Transformation

Expanded focus to comprehensive digital transformation consulting, helping businesses adapt to rapidly changing technology landscapes.

Today: Looking Ahead

Silicon continues to innovate and expand, with a growing team of experts dedicated to delivering cutting-edge technology solutions.
